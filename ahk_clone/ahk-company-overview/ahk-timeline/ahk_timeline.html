AHK FF&E

AHK has one of the most advanced production premises in the country. AHK produces all distinctive quality furniture and upholstered products in its premises by its qualified, expert team. In its 20.000 sqm facility in Antalya, Turkey. There are production lines for fixed and loose furniture, customized joinery, upholstery workshop and metal furniture.
