Quality

Whilst always aiming to render the best services to its customers, AHK endeavors to continuously improve all the work processes under it.

Our Company policy is to supply products of high quality and maintain customer confidence and satisfaction by high standards of support. In this respect we proudly state that AHK has created and implemented its quality system in such a way as to meet the requirements of the ISO 9001:2015 standard. All the requirements defined in ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System and ISO 14001: 2006 Environmental Management System are duly met by AHK.

On the other hand –AHK holds the certificate of BM TRADA TIMBER FIRE DOORS BS 476- PART:22 for 30, 60, 90 and 120 minutes fire rated doors. Obtaining these certificates AHK has embodied its high quality service and main principle of implementing the best production in the shortest possible time without making concessions to the quality of production.
